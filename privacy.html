Moonlit Psychiatry SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. What data we collect

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Messages you send and receive
  • Delivery and opt‑in / opt‑out status

2. How we use your data

  • To coordinate appointments and clinical care
  • To send reminders or educational material you request

3. How we protect your data

  • Messages are stored in our HIPAA‑compliant electronic medical record
  • We do not sell or share your data for marketing

4. Your choices

  • Reply STOP to any message to cancel SMS communications
  • Contact privacy@trymoonlit.com to review or correct your data
